Home Page

On this website restaurants can give away their surplus foods to non-profit organanizations

Restaurants will create a ticket on the event page, and the manager for non-profit will select what they want to pick up on a first come first serve basis

At our core, we are driven by a mission to alleviate hunger and reduce food waste. By redistributing surplus food from restaurants to non-profit organizations, we ensure that no meal goes to waste and that those in need receive the nourishment they deserve. Together, we can create a community where excess food reaches the hungry, fostering a healthier and more sustainable future for all.

Our mission is to combat hunger and food waste by connecting restaurants with surplus food to non-profit organizations that can distribute it to those in need. We believe that no food should be discarded when there are hungry people to feed. Through our efforts, we strive to create a sustainable solution that nourishes communities and reduces waste.

We are dedicated to ending food insecurity and minimizing waste by channeling surplus food from restaurants to those who need it most. Our mission is to ensure that every available resource is used to its fullest potential, providing meals to the hungry and fostering a culture of sustainability and care. Join us in making a difference, one meal at a time.
